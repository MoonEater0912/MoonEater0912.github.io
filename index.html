About Me

Welcome, and thanks for reading. I am Chen Zeyuan, currently a graduate student at Uppsala University, majoring in Social Science. I am interested in quantitative and computational methods. I am now learning causal inference, text analysis and network analysis. I am focused on the research and theories about knowledge production and social movements, and trying combining qual and quant methods to gain insights. I love cooking, reading and language learning. I post related blogs and projects here. Sometimes I will share my recipes.
