This is the research I am currently working on as a visiting student at Aalto University, with Keller Barbara and Anh Duong Nguyen. The idea is motivated by our [previous work](https://mooneater0912.github.io/post/2025/04/07/LargeTeamCollaboration) published on CHI.

Our current findings are details in this [working file](/files/teamsize_workingfile.pdf)). In short, a large team size could contribute (monotonically increasing) to gender disparity in terms of representation, even if there are only unequal gender ratio and choice homophily, without any intentional exclusion or discrimination.
