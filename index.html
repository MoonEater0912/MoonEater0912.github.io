About Me

Welcome, and thanks for reading. I am Chen Zeyuan, currently a student at Uppsala University, majoring in social science. I am interested in computation and data science, as well as the critique theories of software and network. Specifically, I am currently interested in users' collective actions and affectedness in digital media (how they are affected, transformed and reshaped by different types of digital media). Besides, I am also drawn to social movements, resistence, computational methods in social sciences, etc. I post related blogs and projects here. Sometimes I will share my translations (from english/french to chinese) of articles on french philosophy, and sometimes I will share my recipes.
