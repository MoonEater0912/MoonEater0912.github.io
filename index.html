About Me

Welcome, and thanks for reading. I am Chen Zeyuan, currently a first-year master student at Uppsala University, majoring in Social Sciences. My academic interests lie in computational social science, with a particular focus on leveraging textual computation and network analysis to measure and understand the dynamics of knowledge production and social change, enabling causal identification. I am also interested in quantitative health studies, in which field I have been working as a research assistant, with several manuscriptes accepted or under review. Before pursuing social science studies, I earned my undergraduate degree in philosophy and sociology, where I focused on probability, contingency, and cybernetics, and wrote my thesis on these topics.
