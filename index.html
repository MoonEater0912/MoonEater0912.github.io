About Me

Welcome, and thanks for reading.

I am Chen Zeyuan, currently a first-year master student at Uppsala University, majoring in Social Sciences. My academic interests lie in leveraging computational and quantitative methods to measure and understand the dynamics of knowledge production and social change, enabling causal identification. I am particularly interested in the potential driving factors of social inequality (in knowledge representation, health states, etc.). Before pursuing social science studies, I earned my undergraduate degree in philosophy and sociology, where I focused on probability, contingency, and cybernetics, and wrote my thesis on these topics.

This summer I am going to Aalto University for a 3-month research internship in the field of computational social science!
