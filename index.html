About Me

Welcome, and thanks for reading. I am Chen Zeyuan, currently a graduate student at Uppsala University, majoring in Social Science. My academic interests lie in computational social science, with a particular focus on leveraging textual computation and network analysis to measure and understand dynamics of knowledge production and social change, ultimately enabling causal identification. Specifically, I am interested in how computational methods can quantify aspects of 20th-century digital and technological culture, such as the evolving nature of science fiction literature and the writing strategies of digital social movement groups. Additionally, I am investigating how machine learning can facilitate predictions about the life histories and events of different populations. Before pursuing social science studies, I earned my undergraduate degree in philosophy and sociology, where I focused on probability, contingency, and cybernetics, and wrote my thesis on these topics.
