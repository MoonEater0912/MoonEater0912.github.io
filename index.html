About Me

I am Zeyuan Chen (陈 泽远). I'm from a small coastal county in southeastern mainland China. My favorite food includes iced rice, Chaoshan beef hotpot, and luosifen. After one year of studying mathematics, I completed a double bachelor's degree in Philosophy and Sociology at Beijing Normal University. I am currently a master student in Social Sciences at Uppsala University (graduating in 2026).

I am interested in computational approaches to socio-cultural analysis - particularly in developing and using interpretable NLP and network analysis methods to build substantive mappings between textual semantics and social space (between symbolic field and material field). I am currently seeking PhD opportunities in related fields (Fall 2026 or later). My CV can be found here.

Specifically, my research interests include -

  • Computational social science / cultural history
  • Causal inference with network analysis and NLP
  • History of intellectuals and discursive space, particularly in 20th century (France, Germany, Japan, China, etc.)
  • Inequality and its reproduction

Beyond research, I love reading, playing video games, city walking, and learning languages.
